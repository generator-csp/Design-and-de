Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is a farming waste solution?
Farming waste solutions refer to methods of managing and recycling agricultural waste like crop residues, animal manure, and other by-products to produce compost, bioenergy, or organic fertilizers.
2. How can farmers benefit from your service?
Farmers can reduce disposal costs, earn by selling waste, and use organic outputs like compost or biofuel to improve soil health and crop yield.
3. What types of farming waste do you accept?
We accept crop residues, husks, straw, cow dung, poultry litter, and other biodegradable agricultural by-products.
4. Is there any cost involved?
Basic collection is free. Some premium services like on-site composting or biofuel plant support may involve nominal fees.
5. How do I schedule a waste pickup?
You can schedule a pickup through our website’s pickup request form or contact our local partner through the helpline.
